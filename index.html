How It Works

Six steps. Zero friction.

01

Connect Wallet

Connect your wallet or use our API. Access KYC-verified, regulated stablecoins only.

02

Select Currency Pair

Choose from live USD, EUR, and other stablecoin corridors. More pairs launching regularly.

03

Request AMM Quote

Our intelligent router checks the AMM pool depth and slippage vs. oracle pricing. If spread is near-market and slippage is low: execute instantly on the AMM.

04

Auto-Route to RFQ

If AMM slippage is too high, the router automatically requests competing quotes from leading FX liquidity providers. Best price wins.

05

Settle On-Chain

Transaction confirmed on-chain in under 30 seconds. Full immutable audit trail written automatically. No manual reconciliation.

06

Send to Partners

Transfer directly to any business partner via on-chain transfer OR share a BlankFX payment link — just like PayPal or Wise, but on-chain.
